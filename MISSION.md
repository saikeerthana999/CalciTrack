# My Mission: Democratizing Precision Cardiology

### **Motto: Care Ever, Neglect Never**
### *Start Early, Drive Slowly, Reach Safely*

As a 4th-year medical student, I developed **CalciTrack** because I believe high-end heart care shouldn't be a luxury hidden behind the walls of corporate hospitals. My mission is to make precision medicine **affordable, accessible, and available** right at your doorstep.

---

## My Philosophy: The Roadmap to a Healthy Heart

I believe every patient deserves a clear path to health that avoids the trauma and expense of the Emergency Room. I built CalciTrack around three core pillars:

### 1. Start Early (Detection)
In our community, heart disease starts 5–10 years earlier than global averages. We cannot wait for symptoms to appear.
* **My Goal:** I want to identify risk in your 20s and 30s, long before you ever need a hospital bed.
* **The Tool:** By bringing screening to community centers and homes, I ensure we "Start Early" while prevention is still simple and affordable.



### 2. Drive Slowly (Diagnosis)
When a risk is detected, big hospitals often rush patients into expensive, high-stress environments. "Driving Slowly" means we take the time for **precision**.
* **My Goal:** I use data to prevent "Emergency Room anxiety" and unnecessary costs.
* **The Tool:** I integrated biomarkers like $Lp(a)$ and $hs-CRP$. By getting a precise diagnosis today, we avoid the "crash" of a sudden cardiac event and the massive bills that come with it.



### 3. Reach Safely (Destination)
The final goal of my work is a sustainable solution, not a surgery. 
* **My Goal:** A managed, non-emergency path to a long life.
* **The Tool:** CalciTrack connects high-risk individuals directly to specialists. By finding the right path based on the diagnosis now, I ensure my patients "Reach Safely" into a healthy future without the detour to the ER.

---

##  Bringing Precision to the "Doorstep"
I am committed to this model because your pin/zip code or income should never determine your life expectancy. My tool is:

1. **Pocket-Sized:** Expert clinical logic that works anywhere, not just in a billion-dollar facility.
2. **Community-First:** Speaking the language of the people and respecting the local culture of care.
3. **Affordable:** Focused on low-cost prevention today to save families from high-cost crises tomorrow.
